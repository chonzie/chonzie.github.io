Ho Chi Minh Trail

by Motorbike in 10 days

Day 1: Ho Chi Minh → Phan Thiet (quick stop at Vung Tau)

Distance: 230 km – 5 hours

Getting used to the traffic wasn't too bad and even got a chance to resolve an odd noise on the bike at a roadside shop. Couldn't have picked a better place to reset than a pit stop at Thuong Chieu Zen Monastery and Ham Thuan Nam.

Day 2: Phan Thiet → Dalat

Distance: 174 km - 4 hours

High up in the mountains DaLat has a unique micro climate and is cool enough to make their own wine

Day 3: Dalat → Nha Trang

Distance: 135 km - 3 hours

Some of the best curvy and well maintained roads through small and large beach towns.

Day 4: Nha Trang → Quy Nhon

Distance: 236 km - 5 hours

Roads get dustier in-land mud baths are a great way to prepare for the next stretch

Day 5: Quy Nhon → Hoi An

Distance: 291 km - 5 hours 10 minutes

Iconic rice patties and the rice roadside snacks are amazing

Day 6: Hoi An → Hue (pit stop at Da Nang for sightseeing)

Distance: 154 km - 4 hours

Hoi An is a charming town on the river with French, Chinese and Japanese architecture. Colorful lantern boat ride was a magical experience.

Day 7: Hue → Phong Nha cave

Distance: 231 km - 5 hours

Hue has a rich cultural heritage with unqie cousine and beautiful palaces

Day 8: Phong Nha cave → Pho Chau

Distance: 182 km - 4 hours

Waking up for the boat early is the best way to take in the scenery in peace and quiet

Day 9: Pho Chau → Cuc Phuong National Park

Distance: 316 km - 6 hours

Roads away from the coast are much dustier and shortcuts are not always paved. Lots of logging trucks but we watched a beautiful thunderstorm over the national park.

Day 10: Cuc Phuong → Hanoi

Distance: 139 km - 3 hours

Great cocktails, cousine, art and music
